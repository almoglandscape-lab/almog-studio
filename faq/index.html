FAQ

Questions, answered.

Who is Almog?

A landscape artist and outdoor designer based in Israel. He composes private gardens, rooftops and outdoor spaces — honest places made of stone, green and light — through Almog Studio.

What does Almog Studio do?

Garden design, outdoor and rooftop design, site-specific garden art, and long-term garden management — for private residences and estates across Israel.

Where does the studio work?

Across Israel — private residences, penthouses and small estates. Travel for the right project is welcome.

What makes the gardens different?

Every garden begins with the people who'll live in it, and is composed by golden-ratio proportion with native, climate-fit planting. The aim is a place that changes how you feel in it — and ages well.

How long does a garden take?

A clear concept usually takes a few weeks once the site is understood. The garden itself matures over seasons by design — planted to grow into itself rather than peak on day one.

How do I start?

Write to hello@almog.studio, or message on WhatsApp or Instagram. The first step is always a conversation about the place and the people who'll live in it.
