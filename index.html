About Almog — Landscape Artist in Israel

Almog is an Israeli landscape artist and outdoor designer. He composes private gardens, rooftops and outdoor spaces with a focus on native Mediterranean planting, stone, water and light. His practice is rooted in golden-ratio composition, Fibonacci spacing and a psychology-led understanding of how people live in a place. Almog Studio works across Israel on residences, penthouses and small estates. The studio name "Almog" means "coral" in Hebrew — a living, slow-growing structure the work is named after.
