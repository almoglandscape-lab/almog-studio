About Almog — Landscape Artist

Almog is a landscape artist and outdoor designer. He composes private gardens, rooftops and outdoor spaces with a focus on native Mediterranean planting, stone, water and light. His practice is rooted in golden-ratio composition, Fibonacci spacing and a psychology-led understanding of how people live in a place. Almog Studio works on private residences, penthouses and estates — from the Mediterranean to wherever the work calls. The studio name "Almog" means "coral" in Hebrew — a living, slow-growing structure the work is named after.
