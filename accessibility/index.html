Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 2026

Almog Studio is committed to making almog.studio accessible to all people, including those with disabilities. We aim to meet the requirements of Israeli Standard 5568 (based on WCAG 2.1 Level AA) and the Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities Law, 5758–1998.

Our Commitment

We have taken the following steps to make this website accessible:

  • All images include descriptive alternative text
  • Navigation is accessible via keyboard (Tab, Enter, Escape)
  • A "Skip to content" link is provided at the top of each page
  • Colour contrast meets WCAG AA standards throughout the site
  • The site is usable without JavaScript for core content
  • Decorative elements are hidden from screen readers with aria-hidden
  • Interactive elements have clear focus indicators
  • The navigation menu is fully operable with keyboard and screen readers
  • Videos in the hero section are muted by default and contain no essential audio

Known Limitations

We are continuously working to improve accessibility. Known limitations at this time:

  • The horizontal project carousel may not be fully accessible on all screen readers — use the "All works" link to view all projects on a standard page
  • Some SVG illustrations do not yet have extended text descriptions

Feedback and Contact

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on this website, please let us know. We take all reports seriously and aim to respond within 5 business days.

Email: hello@almog.studio
WhatsApp: Message us on WhatsApp

Enforcement

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Israeli Equal Employment Opportunities Commission or submit a complaint via the Ministry of Justice accessibility portal.

This statement was prepared in June 2026 and will be reviewed annually.
