Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Almog Studio ("we", "us") operates the website almog.studio. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you contact us — such as your name, email address, or phone number sent via the contact form, email, or WhatsApp.

We also collect non-personal usage data through analytics tools (such as Microsoft Clarity) to understand how visitors use the site. This may include your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on each page.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and communicate about potential projects
  • To improve our website based on how it is used
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to analyse traffic and usage. Microsoft Clarity records mouse movements, clicks, and scrolls for usability analysis. You can opt out by disabling JavaScript in your browser, though this may affect site functionality.

Data Retention

We retain contact information only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or fulfil a service agreement. Analytics data is retained per the third-party provider's default retention settings.

Your Rights (GDPR & Israeli Privacy Law)

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Israel's Privacy Protection Law, 5741–1981 and its regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to our processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@almog.studio.

Third-Party Links

Our website may link to Instagram and WhatsApp. These services have their own privacy policies and we are not responsible for their practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Questions? Email us at hello@almog.studio.
